How to Play

  • Each player gets a secret card showing which Tikis to get to the top of the stack.
  • On your turn, play one Action Card to rearrange the Tiki Tower.
  • After all players take a turn, score points based on your secret card and the top 3 Tikis.
  • Heat Blast removes the bottom Tiki from the round (can't use on first turn).
  • First player to reach 35 points wins the game!
  • Top Tiki (position 1) scores the most. Positions 2 & 3 score less.
